Design identity

A dark, instrument-panel aesthetic for precision agricultural robotics. The accent green reads as the crop and the "go" signal; danger red is the bruise bound; amber warns; blue carries data and edge/network context. Three typefaces separate roles: a geometric display face for headlines, a humanist sans for prose, and a monospace for every number, label, and equation so quantitative content stays legible and aligned.

Color tokens

The complete semantic palette. Every asset draws color only from these tokens - no raw values appear outside the theme block - which is what makes the suite recolorable and consistent. Per-asset palettes (FSM stages, category hues, fidelity-ladder cells) extend this set locally.
